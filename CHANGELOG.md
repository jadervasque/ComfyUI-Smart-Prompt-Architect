## [Unreleased]

### Added

- Initial project structure, package metadata, community documents, and quality configuration.
- Field mode `custom` with a per-section free-text editor, deterministic manifest provenance, and
  backward-compatible configuration schema 1.1.
- Catalog V2 with 81 segmented packs, 69 atomic logical libraries, 5,184 original semantic
  options, 15,552 deterministic wording variants, pack-level safety metadata, and 15 official
  profiles.
- Hierarchical family selection plus distinct strict, balanced, creative, dataset, and sequential
  strategies.
- Modular negative prompts, adaptive framing density, Catalog V2 manifests, semantic audits,
  wildcard taxonomy audits, diversity metrics, and performance reporting.

### Fixed

- Keep the visible `identity_lock`, Basic editor checkbox, and `Groups > identity` lock synchronized
  when opening and saving the structured prompt editor.
