# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and empathetic.
- Give and accept technical feedback in good faith.
- Focus on what is best for users and the community.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing sensitive information.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, sexualized attention, and deliberate
disruption are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's security reporting channel when it
becomes available. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this policy. Publisher contact details
will be added before public release.

This policy is inspired by the Contributor Covenant 2.1 and will be reviewed before public beta.
